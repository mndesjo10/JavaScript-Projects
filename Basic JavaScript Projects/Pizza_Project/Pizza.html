About Galaxy Pizza

Founded in 2015, Galaxy Pizza has been serving the South Shields community with out-of-this-world pizzas for over a decade. Our mission is to bring cosmic flavours to your table with every slice.

What makes our pizza special? We are use only the freshest ingredients sourced from local suppliers, hand-tossed dough made fresh daily, and our secret family sauce recipe that's been passed down for generations.

Our team of pizza artisans are passionate about creating the perfect pie - whether you're craving a classic Margherita or an adventurous specialty pizza like our famous Black Hole Meat Feast.

We believe pizza should be an experience, not just a meal. That's why we're created a space where families, friends, and pizza lovers of all ages can gather to enjoy delicious food in a fun, space-themed atmosphere.
